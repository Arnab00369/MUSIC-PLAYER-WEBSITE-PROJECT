Arijit Singh

Indian Singer and Composer

6,39,000 Followers
3,60,000 Monthly Listeners
Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian singer and music composer. The recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award and seven Filmfare Awards, he has recorded songs in several Indian languages.
Singh began his career when he participated in the contemporary reality show, Fame Gurukul in 2005, but did not receive widespread recognition until the release of "Tum Hi Ho" and "Chahun Main Ya Naa" in 2013. He was declared one of the most-streamed Indian artist of 2020, 2021 and 2022. He is the 4th most followed artist globally and one of the most followed Asian.
